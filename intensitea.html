Intensitea

Identity and packaging for a concept tea brand inspired by the diversity of tea. All true tea comes from the same plant; whether the leaves are destined to become a delicate white tea or an aromatic pu-erh depends on when they are picked and how they are processed.

The spectrum from light to dark is the main concept behind this design for a sampler set of teas. The brush strokes are an extension of the colour concept, and they also resemble the delicate twisted form of dried tea leaves.
