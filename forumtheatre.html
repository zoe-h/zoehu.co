Forum Theatre

Brand revitalization for Forum Theatre, with patterns and colours inspired by the building’s beautiful architecture.

The logo retains the Forum’s iconic gryphon, but adds elegance. The interwoven geometry echoes the beautiful Moorish patterns that adorn the theatre. These patterns were also recreated to use as supporting visual elements.
The building was originally designed as an 'atmostpheric theatre,' with lavish ornamentation and a gorgeous blue ceiling. It was designed to transport the audience to an exotic faraway land. The visual identity seeks to create that sense of dreaminess and the surreal.
