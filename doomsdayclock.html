Doomsday Clock

We often forget that websites are live and temporary. The aim of this project was to capture the spirit of 'time' and break the fourth wall, so to speak. By making the site responsive to the user's time, the subject matter becomes more real and tangible.

Visit the site

The Doomsday Clock counts down the minutes to 'midnight,' with midnight being global destruction. In 2017 the Clock was set to two and a half minutes, the closest time since 1953.
The website itself simulates the end of the world; every night from 11:57:30PM the site gradually self-destructs.
